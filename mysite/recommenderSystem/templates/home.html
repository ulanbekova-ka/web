Firstly, do you know what is mood reading?

Mood reading is what it sounds like: it’s a type of reading method where you decide what to read based on your current mood. It means you can read multiple books at once. Of course, it is not only about literature.

Our mood can affect you as a reader...

If your mood and your current read are at odds, your enjoyment of that read will likely suffer, and you could end up feeling frustrated. For example, if you’re feeling stressed out or overwhelmed, you’ll likely have trouble staying focused when reading a complex story.

Correct choice of media can help you to regulate your emotions!

Yes, it can. Probably you as many of other people use music to cope with some strong emotions. Reading proper books and watching movies can work too.
